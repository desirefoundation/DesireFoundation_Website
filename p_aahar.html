Aahar

The Beginning

We see places lit up, with glee and we know, it's the time around Dusshera, Durga Puja and Navratre!" Just, when whole of the country indulged itself into the festivities in the year 2017, Desire Foundation, had different plans. After great efforts, tireless hours of planning and blood-sweat hard work, we came up with this great idea, helping the kids celebrate this occasion with equal happiness! On the 30th of September and 1st of October, Desire Foundation had organized a two-day Food Donation drive by the name "Ahaar" for the blind and homeless children across Mumbai and Durgapur. Aahar in Mumbai, was in association with BOX8, which sponsored freshly cooked, warm meal boxes, and we volunteered its distribution amongst the deserving. This was successfully completed, feeding 250 children in Mumbai and Navi Mumbai. We have always lived by the motto, that basic rights, like food, water and education should be accessible by all. And during the biggest festive season in India, they didn't back off from their responsibilities, but tried and successfully ensured a proper meal for the underprivileged kids of Mumbai. After being fed with good, warm meal, the shine in the eyes of those kids were greater than the greatest treasure. Nobody can, and should be left to enjoy with an empty stomach. We are a group of very close knit, and passionate youngsters who realise our responsibility towards the society and move forward with an initiative to change and revolutionize the society, are ready to take up more such challenges.

Testimonials

It was our desire to do something not done before. It was our desire to help few underprivileged and see them smile. As days passed, more friends joined us and the joy of on-boarding big names like Box8 can't be explained in words. They sponsored the food packets for all the orphans we planned to feed. To our surprise big publicity groups joined hands to promote our event in big banners across Mumbai. It was magical. On one hand, we could see the kids eat a good meal and on the other our name was displayed in the heart of the commercial capital of India. I will cherish these moments throughout my life.

Trisha Srivastava, Alumni

When the innocent kids in this beautiful world are neglected, when they are forced to the shadows because they are not a part of the conventional society, then organisations like Desire Foundation & Andal Pariwar stand by them. They are brought back to the light, so that they can spend a healthy and normal life just like everyone else. Andal Pariwar has changed the lives of 25 kids belonging to the underprivileged background by including them in the conventional society with providing food, health, education and entertainment. The key ingredient for making a person honest and righteous is education. Desire Foundation has helped in this educational aspect along with the other daily needs of the kids. When all around people were immersed in the festivities of Durga puja during the year 2017, Desire Foundation in collaboration with Andal Pariwar, arranged a sumptuous Aahar(meal) for the kids. On this day the kids were taken to a temple. They played and laughed against the serene backdrop of the sanctuary and we prepared a meal for them. Our goal was to see the smile on their innocent faces. Their happiness knew no bounds. We hope that these kids have a happy and peaceful future. They evolve as humans in the true sense of the term. We wish them all the best.

Sushmita Ghoshal, Board Member
