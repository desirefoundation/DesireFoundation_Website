Udaan

How it started?

It was a long dream to have the small kids belonging to the slums and nearby areas perform on stage. They generally don't get enough opportunities or recognition to showcase their talent. With this intent, and also as a fundraiser event we thought of organizing an open mic event. In one half of the event kids performed the rhymes they knew and loved. And in the other half we had artists from all over the city recite poetry over their thoughts on education.

Preparation for the big day!!

(Bhubaneswar Poetry Club). Therefore, our team was pretty much new to the idea of organizing poetry slams. We divided ourselves into two groups. One group took care of the registration process, spreading the word about the campaign, venue hunting, and eventually venue booking, arranging the equipment’s like camera, mic, sound system. The other group took care of preparing the small kids for their first event of that would help them be more confident to speak up in a crowd. After much discussion the theme for the event was decided - Back to School. People from all over the city, of various age groups participated in the event. We also arranged a music band to serenade us with their live music. This event was organised with the motive to encourage these young minds to think beyond and run towards their aspirations. To give them the hope that dreaming big doesn’t come with a price tag. This was a small step towards the ultimate goal. We look forward to collaborate with organisations like the BPC in the future to conduct many more similar events.
