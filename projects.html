OUR PROJECT

ADHYAYAN


Adhyayan, which means, to study, is exactly what this project is all about. Adhyayan strives to provide equal educational opportunities to the children of EWS of the society under the ambit of RTE Act 12(1)(c) by using the youth as a resource. This section under the RTE Act mandates 25% reservation for the children from economically and socially weaker sections in private primary schools free of cost.

KHWAISH-E-SIKSHA


Books for primary standard students from a financially weaker section of the society were arranged by exchanging books from all over the community that were not required by the people anymore. This was a gleaming initiative to help the children belonging to the economically feeble section of the society to avail quality books free of cost.

AAHAR


This year, Desire foundation took a step forward with its first initiative in Mumbai through "AAHAR". We were able to provide freshly cooked meals to over 300 blind and homeless kids, and children belonging to the underprivileged sections of the society. In association with BOX8-Desi Meals, Desire Foundation was successful in providing meals, covering areas of Navi Mumbai and Mumbai especially the slums in Kopar Khairane.

LITER OF LIGHT


In 2015, Desire Foundation took steps to illuminate houses in the slums of Bhubaneshwar which were still in darkness. Originated in Brazil, this is an eco-friendly and long-lasting way to light homes! Our project in 2014 turned into an international initiative by Liter Of Light global in 2017.
