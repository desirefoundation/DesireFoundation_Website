UDAAN


Poets have the gift of expression. The power of words, metaphors and stories cannot be underestimated in the context of how they influence a crowd. When creativity meets social commitment, somehow the sound is louder and the intent, clearer. We, the people of Desire are always on the lookout for people who can contribute to our cause in whatever little way possible. What better than collaborating with the most creative minds of the city to create an afternoon full of poetry and music? Hence, we talked about childhood, schooldays, had an awesome musical performance and last but not the least, some beautiful rhymes from the kids.

DIWALI


The members of Desire Foundation went to Mahimanagar slum in Bhubaneshwar to spread happiness and joy on the occasion of Diwali. The idea was to celebrate Diwali in a non- conventional approach while making it jolly for the children in the slum as well as be eco-friendly. Card making competition was organised and crayon sets were given out as prizes. Food packets and chocolates were also distributed among those 50 children. Diyas were also distributed to 40 families.

RICE BUCKET CHALLENGE


Originally initiated in Hyderabad, Desire took part in the Rice Bucket Challenge on 29th August 2014, donating 61 Kgs of rice to 61 families. Further we nominated KAASH, NSS, Parivartan and RON. It was a bright-eyed initiative that addressed the issue ‘absence of proper food for the deprived section of our community’. Such initiatives might bring a significant change in their nutritional requirements.

EK NAYI UMEED


It is an initiative to financially help the underprivileged children. We achieved this with the help of crowdfunding and communicated with various organizations for sponsorship. We completed our 1st phase of Ek Nayi Umeed on February 2017. In phase 1, we helped a girl named Tarini who aspires to become a doctor in the future. We helped her through a crowdfunding website, Ketto and supported her to take a firm step towards her dream.The story of Tarini was then uploaded on Ketto. We marketized the donation link and collected around Rs. 12,000. A fixed amount of money is given to her as scholarship.
