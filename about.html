About Us

DESIRE FOUNDATION


Desire foundation is a govt. registered trust which aims to empower underprivileged sections of the society. We are an organization committed to social upliftment through engaging of youth and creating a business where the motive is not just profit but to bring the marginalised communities into mainstream. We are currently focused on fulfilling the fourth Sustainable Development Goal â€“ Quality Education. Our current project Adhyayan is focused on education with primary education as the main concern. In the past we have created considerable impact through our events like Liter of Light, Aahar, Khwaish-e-Shiksha etc in West Bengal, Maharashtra and Odisha.

MISSION


To bring equal educational opportunities to the disadvantaged sections of the society by utilizing youth as a resource, thereby making them socially responsible and increasing access to education. We aim at a more wholesome society where no one is discriminated on the basis of caste, gender and financial background to avail the basic right, education. We also target to utilise the exquisite minds of the youth of our country by bringing them together for a greater good while being instrumental in their growth. Our goal is to bridge the gap between the wealthy and the impoverished sections of the community. Every educated child represents a brighter future for our country. Every socially responsible youth represents a more informed and educated country.

VISION


A society with equal access to the basic humanitarian rights and educational opportunities irrespective of caste, creed, gender and financial background. We envision a community where not even a single person should feel the lack of basic human rights food, clothing, shelter and education. A community where no one has to fight for availing nutritious food, for having a roof to sleep under, for acquiring quality education and receiving a chance to a radiant future. A community where there is no price tag on dreaming and achieving.

HOW AND WHEN DESIRE STARTED


Desire Foundation was established by a group of like-minded college students wanting to give back to the society. We started with the idea to tackle the need of basic amenities like proper shelter for the economically feeble section of the society. We lit up the houses in slums of Bhubaneswar through an eco-friendly approach. Then broadened our focus by addressing the issue of availability of proper food for the underprivileged section of our society. Further diversifying our horizons and shifting our focus majorly on their educational rights. Education in our opinion is a more holistic approach to tackle all the above-mentioned issues. Education creates the opportunity for leading a more upstanding lifestyle by getting proper food, shelter and clothing. We brought together the brightest and sharpest minds of the youth in our community to be a part of this transformation. The foundation started in the year 2014.
