AN EXUBERANT TALE

Debangshu Chakraborty
20 Feb 2021 • 2 minutes

"Some stories are meant to be shared,

While some stay in shades of unpredictability hidden.

Some instances are meant to be aired,

While some memories hold on and their feelings stay within. "

I started on a note of passion exploration this long run, 

Never thought it could have been so much fun. 

From knowing unknowns and new faces as I joined onboard, 

To making friends and memories I recall when I'm bored. 

The passion for writing fueled the process of joining here, 

Not just an organisation but, Desire Foundation provided me the much needed spotlight here. 

And soon did I realize that this passion could help someone overcome their fear. 

Fears of seclusion, racism, stereotypical thinking that chains their minds from shining bright. 

That moment I thought why not be a much needed help in their fight. 

Through out the journey I too witnessed some ups and some instances that shook me down,

Just to learn from those experiences, carve out a better myself and add them as feathers on my crown. 

Writing captions, blogs and stories were a task I was use to even before ,

But now I had a reason to help somebody fly high in the sky and soar. 

The feeling of helping and catering to minds that were capable yet pushed back, 

Made me emotionally understanding and helped me add up experiences on my stack. 

Today as I see back and wonder what I really worked on and achieved after all, 

I realize it's just a bunch of happy faces with an enlightened future that makes me proud of my contribution after all.

Debangshu Chakraborty
