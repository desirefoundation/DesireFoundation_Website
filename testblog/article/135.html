Rahul's COVID Journey: Chapter 3

Saptaswa Munsi
11 May 2021 • 8 minutes

Rajeev has been keeping in touch with Rahul regularly now to understand the pulse of how things are at Rahul’s end. Rajeev has been extremely
busy today with his college lectures and it was only very late at night that he could wrap up his work. He knows Rahul must have fallen
asleep, but an elder brother as he is, decides to drop him a message on whatsapp.

Rajeev– Hey kiddo, how are you today? Sorry couldn’t call you, exams are in a week! Hope you are well asleep.

Rahul– Hi Bhaiya! All good, things are a little better now. Some of our neighbouring families are terribly struck with Covid and we are trying to help them out by searching for whatever resources we can.

Rajeev– How are you searching for resources? Rahul – Bhaiya for now my friends and I are mainly Utilising Messaging Channels to find Covid Resources

Rajeev– Very good brother, it is always better to Share Verified Resources only because it saves time for the person who is in a state or emergency.

Rahul– True bhaiya. What about you? I saw your social organisation organised a couple of events?

Rajeev– Yes, we wanted to spread the word to everyone that Vaccination is Safe and that Everyone Should opt for Vaccination as soon as they get a chance. Did you Register Your Family For Vaccination?

Rahul – Yes! We still have not been able to book a date. Any News on Vaccine Availability?

Rajeev– Kiddo, as you know we are a population of around 130 crores. It will take some time to normalise the supply. Why don’t you register here
to get notified on the availability of slots near you? Till then do you remember what all I asked you to do to keep your health better?

Rahul – Yes Bhaiya. I read how Obesity is a Huge Factor for Serious Covid Illness

1)Hydration – Drink plenty of water

2)Nutrition – Have a balanced diet with optimum Protein intake and Green Vegetables for Vitamins and Minerals

3)Motion – Walk around, water your plants, do Yoga or any body weight workout that you like

4)Rest – Make sure to sleep for an optimum 7 to 8 hours at night

Rajeev– Very good my boy! Yes these will help you stay strong from internally, and in case you need to go out?

Rahul– Double Masks!

Rajeev– Absolutely. Don’t forget to check out the COVID Airborne guidelines.

Rahul– But bhaiya I have told Mumma not to go out anymore. I am using Apps for Grocery Ordering and also Apps for Medicine Ordering.

Rahul– Proud of you! There is no alternative to staying at home. Staying Home Saves Lives! Remember, recovery rate is improving and we’ll definitely beat this virus too, like any other enemy. Chalo I’ll call you tomorrow, you get your 8 hours sleep

Rahul– Bye bye Bhaiya!
