About Us

DESIRE FOUNDATION

Desire Foundation is a govt. reg. trust which aims to empower children from vulnerable sections of the society by providing them equal opportunities for education.

Currently, we are working on implementation of RTE Sec 12(1)(c) in Bhubaneshwar through our project Adhyayan which has Successfully completed phase 1 ,2 & 3. Phase 3 completed on April 2019. Over these five years we have interacted with 1030 children, 30 schools and over 150 young students.
